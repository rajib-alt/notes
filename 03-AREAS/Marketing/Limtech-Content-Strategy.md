# 🏢 Limtech Content Strategy

## Products

| Product | Core benefit | Audience |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| SFA | Daily planning, field tracking, memo generation | Sales reps, managers |
| CRM | Lead management, pipeline, follow-up | Sales teams, SME owners |
| ERP | Warehouse, VAT, HRM, distributor mgmt | Mid-size businesses |

## ✍️ Copy principles
1. **Empathetic, not surveillance** — help salespeople, don't police them
2. **Short hook** — first line must stop the scroll
3. **Pain-point led** — problem first, solution second
4. **Bangla-first** for Facebook; English for LinkedIn
5. **No jargon** — write like a helpful colleague

## Brand voice
**Use:** সহজ · স্মার্ট · দ্রুত · টিম · লক্ষ্য
**Avoid:** নজরদারি · নিয়ন্ত্রণ (framed negatively)
